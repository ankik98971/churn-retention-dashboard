# Retention Action Plan: Ranked Recommendations

**Planning estimates — for stakeholder review and pilot scoping**

---

## #1 — Contract-Upgrade Incentive Campaign

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target segment** | Month-to-month customers with tenure > 6 months |
| **Intervention** | Offer 15% discount or premium service bundle for upgrading to annual contract |
| **Estimated cost** | $15–25 per contacted customer |
| **Estimated revenue saved** | $180–360 per converted customer / year |
| **Estimated ROI** | 7–24× |
| **Rationale** | Month-to-month contracts churn at 3–4× the rate of annual contracts. Locking in even a subset of this segment dramatically stabilizes recurring revenue. |
| **Success metric** | Upgrade conversion rate > 20%; reduction in month-to-month churn > 5 percentage points |
| **Pilot duration** | 6 weeks |

---

## #2 — Proactive Outreach for High-Charge, Low-Tenure Segment

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target segment** | Customers paying >$70/month with tenure < 12 months |
| **Intervention** | Welcome call at 30 days + usage tips + loyalty preview at 90 days |
| **Estimated cost** | $8–12 per touch (email + call) |
| **Estimated revenue saved** | $840+ per retained customer / year |
| **Estimated ROI** | 70–105× |
| **Rationale** | High monthly charges combined with low tenure indicate price sensitivity and low switching cost. Early human touch reduces buyer's remorse and builds habit. |
| **Success metric** | 90-day retention rate in segment > 85% |
| **Pilot duration** | 8 weeks |

---

## #3 — Add-On Service Bundling (Security + Support)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target segment** | All customers without OnlineSecurity or TechSupport |
| **Intervention** | Bundle security + tech support at 20% discount vs. à la carte |
| **Estimated cost** | $5–10 per customer (marginal cost of digital features) |
| **Estimated revenue saved** | $120–240 per retained customer / year |
| **Estimated ROI** | 12–48× |
| **Rationale** | Customers with security and support add-ons show measurably lower churn — they have "more to lose" by leaving. Bundling increases stickiness and perceived value. |
| **Success metric** | Attach rate > 30% in target segment; churn reduction > 3 percentage points |
| **Pilot duration** | 10 weeks |

---

## Implementation Priority

1. **Week 1–2:** Stakeholder alignment on cost assumptions and pilot success criteria
2. **Week 3–4:** Build campaign lists from dashboard filters; draft creative/call scripts
3. **Week 5+:** Launch pilots in parallel where possible; track weekly metrics on this dashboard
4. **Week 12:** Review results, refine assumptions, and recommend scale or pivot
